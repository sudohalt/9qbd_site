Welcome to 9qbd, you are probably curious and would like to know the meaning of 9qbd. Well let me make your quest short; There is nothing special about 9qbd at the moment. Is just a name we chose for our company after brain storming. Initially, we thought of choosing a name that had a meaning tied to it, but then we came to the conclusion that a name is an identity, it doesn't need to have a special meaning at the beginning, But rather it's the person or company that gives the name a special meaning.

Right now, I am sure you can think of names of athletes, religions leaders, top companies, and politicians. These names that you’re thinking of originally were unknown, but because of what they have accomplished is the reason why you know them

Though the name of our company doesn’t have any meaning at the moment, we hope that it will soon because of what we achieve as team for our customers
